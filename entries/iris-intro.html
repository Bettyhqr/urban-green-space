URBAN GREEN
SPACE

Name
Iris, known scientifically as "Iris," belongs to the Iridaceae family.
Features
Appearance: Irises have intricate, elegant blooms with petals that can be purple, blue, yellow, or white, often marked with beautiful veining or patterns. Scent: Some varieties of iris have a sweet, distinct fragrance, similar to violets. Uses: Irises are used in floral arrangements and some varieties are also sources of orris root, used in perfumery. Season: Bloom in late spring to early summer.
Where I found it
I found irises blooming at the New York Botanical Garden, adding vivid colors to the garden beds.
