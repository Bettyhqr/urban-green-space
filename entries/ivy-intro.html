URBAN GREEN
SPACE

Name
Ivy, known scientifically as "Hedera," belongs to the Araliaceae family.
Features
Appearance: Ivy has distinctive green, lobed leaves that are often glossy, growing in a trailing or climbing form. Scent: Ivy itself does not have a notable fragrance, but its greenery adds a lush, natural feel to its environment. Uses: Ivy is often used as ground cover or on walls for its decorative appeal. It also has traditional medicinal uses. Season: Ivy is evergreen, maintaining its foliage year-round.
Where I found it
I saw ivy growing along the stone walls of the Cloisters Museum in Fort Tryon Park, lending a historical and romantic atmosphere to the area.
