SPACE

Name
Tulip, known scientifically as "Tulipa," belongs to the Liliaceae family.
Features
Appearance: Tulips have cup-shaped flowers with smooth, vibrant petals, ranging in colors from red, yellow, pink, to variegated patterns. Scent: Most tulips have a light, pleasant fragrance, though some varieties are more fragrant than others. Uses: Tulips are popular in gardens and are often used as cut flowers to brighten homes and events. Season: Bloom in spring, typically from March to May.
Where I found it
I saw tulips in abundance at the Brooklyn Botanic Garden, especially during their annual spring bloom.
